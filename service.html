Fun-filled adventures await

Welcoming guests with its bright red tiles, Indochine-style architecture and an outdoor swimming pool of nearly 5,000sqm, WonderLand offers an experience journey full of typical natural flavors of the pearl island. Unmissable experiences here include savouring a seafood buffet with live music and magnificent sunset views at Pepper restaurant by the sea, and enjoying a unique Balinese massage at a spa hut above the lake.
